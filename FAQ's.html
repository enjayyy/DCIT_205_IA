Frequently Asked Questions

  • We offer Bachelor's, Master's, and Ph.D. programs in Computer Science. Our undergraduate program includes various specializations to suit your interests.

  • To apply, please visit our Admissions page on the website. It provides detailed information on application procedures, requirements, and deadlines.

  • Prerequisites vary by program. You can find detailed information about specific course prerequisites in the program's admission requirements.

  • Yes, we offer scholarships and financial aid. Visit our Admissions or Financial Aid page for details on available opportunities and application processes.

  • The department encourages student involvement in research. You can explore ongoing projects and connect with faculty members who share your research interests. Check our Research page for more information.

  • We have state-of-the-art computer labs equipped with the latest software and hardware. Visit our Facilities page for details on specific labs and resources.

  • Yes, we work closely with industry partners to provide internship opportunities. Our Career Services page has information on internships, co-op programs, and career development.

  • Explore our Student Organizations page to learn about various clubs and organizations. Information on meetings and events is regularly updated.

  • We provide career counseling, job placement assistance, and host career fairs and networking events. Visit our Career Services page for more details.

  • Yes, faculty members are available for office hours. Check the Contact Us page for individual contact information or reach out to the department office for assistance in scheduling appointments.

  • Keep an eye on our News and Events page for the latest updates. You can also subscribe to our newsletter for regular notifications.
